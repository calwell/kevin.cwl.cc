Time to give thanks...

Fri 21 01 2011

There are those that will TAKE from you - all they do is TAKE as much as they can get. I want to Give Thanks to those that GIVE, give "real" love, give time, give energy, give in, give it all they can for a better day - even if they don't even know why. I thank all those (here, and passed on) who have made my own, and other people's lives better.
It's not often in style to appreciate and thank those that may not be happily in your life, or those that may have passed on. But, I say, this never goes out of style. So, I say thanks.
- Thank You, Mrs Walker for being such an amazing teacher and guidance counselor
- Thank You, Father, you taught me many things - many things you don't even know
- Thank You, Mother, for showing me the other side wasn't so bad, giving me a place to learn values
- Thank You, Crystal, Cara, and Jessica, for showing me that the story can be a happy story
- Thank You Sick Kids, for the miracle you performed - and all of your miracles ([Donate!][])
- Thank You, Derek. Just for being you. The empty space without you will never be replaced
- Thank You, Friends, all of you know who you are. You have made me a better person - even though we live far away, I'm glad we can be in touch

- Thank You, person who let me take that lane on the highway yesterday - you'll never know how much I needed that


.. And Thank You anyone else that should be, and will be on this list.
