White Cowbell - Horseshoe Tavern - March 8, 2008

Fri 21 01 2011

My lord, it just so happens we push an all-time snowfall record on the day the Cowbell come home. I was not going to let a little thing like snow get in my way! I did not get to see last night's show in Hamilton - and I missed all of the western tour.. So I was more than a little bummed...

I get there after two hours of driving, already everyone but my sister had dropped out of the night's proceedings, and to make matters worse - I have to wait in some sort of lineup? I thought "What is this? Do they not know who I am?". Well, they didn't, and I waited about 30mins to get in, the lads had already finished the opening and a song.. I was so glad to be back inside the Casa Cowbell for the first time this year!

This was a packed house! The fans were pumped! it also appeared to be a ticket with 7 other bands (as one of the very friendly bouncers would explain). I was so much more in tune with things here than the last time. The Jager girls were occasionally circulating around the stage refreshing the front-row devotees, I was stuck a few rows back double-fisting a couple Stella's...

Bubba would do an uncharacteristically perfect rendition of "Tear You a New One" - practice! The dancing girl looked great, she's lost weight, but Sgt Rock seems to have gained facial hair - that makes them even? You tell me. "Put The South In Your Mouth" gets interactive and then the boys are whisked off... Seriously, I must have gotten whiplash with how fast they spit!

I did get to say hi at various moment, I had to let Charles and the others know I had attended and then I was off myself. The very friendly and drunk lady I met at the stage was all too happy to share the she'd be married in a few days. Congratulations to you!!

It was a short one, yes - but it was sweet to see the WCO again and I look forward to this year's adventures!! What's next?
