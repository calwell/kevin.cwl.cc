Panama Backpacker Style - 6

Fri 21 01 2011

[![image][]][]

Day 13 - Penonomé
Waking up this morning the distinct sound of partying outside was really cool. I was told that this Carnival goes four days - all day, all night - and doesn't stop. I decided to walk towards the sounds, I had to remember where I was.. No street signs here to help. Gathering some landmarks I work backwards with these:

  1. MUSEO HNOS ARIAS MADRID, turn left
  2. Corp. el sol, turn right

I get to see the local Internet cafe - where the access is slow and they charge .60c an hour, by far the cheapest access in town. The one thing that is super annoying though, is that they filtered suspect "Porn" sites. I would be in Facebook and click on a friend's profile and a filtering message comes up and KILLS the web browser and all the tabs I have open! Brutal to say the least. That is good enough amusement for a couple hours, I then grab lunch at Pio Pio, and head back towards all the action in town. What action indeed! On the main street in town there are about 8 tanker trucks lined up and down road - the sole purpose of these being to spray water on the people below dancing in the sun. It was great, I went in and got wet and sprayed myself, I even got the chance to hop on top of a tanker and spray the people below! Wow, that was amazing. So many happy people.

I ran into Tania and we went to the river where the Queen of the Carnival was due to arrive - I ended up very tired so I went back for an afternoon nap... I slept a hell of a lot too .. Waking up sometime around 8pm, Tania and I walked back into town to eat and get on the Internet.. The party was in full swing but I was not drinking tonight.. Tomorrow we have to travel to panama city and stay our last day at the voyager hostel.

Day 14 - Back to Panama City
Its 7:20am in Penonomé.. Everyone here stayed up all night - so everyone is sleeping while I'm thinking about breakfast! I leave the house and headed for a walk - I walk up to the local Pio Pio store and ordered two scambled egg combos (huevo revoulto) because I was so hungry. I took the sausages back for Max, the family dog, to eat, and he loved them. I then worked out the plans for travel with Tania - we would leave right away on the bus and get to the hostel around 5pm. When we got there we met up with some really cool Canadian/Dutch/Americans and headed back out for carnival.. This version of carnival was much different than Penonomé - this was Pamama City and it was massive!! People in this (possibly million strong) crowd were throwing confetti on each other (this was the small circle that is cut out of paper with a three-hole punch).. They were also spraying some sort of foam out of cans to.. I was constantly impressed by how nice and welcoming everyone was ... It was a great party and I ended up drunk and had to stubble home on this one..

Day 15 - Homeward Bound
Waking up after last nights craziness was cool - I did have to struggle with confetti all over my body though , that was a real pain in the ass! We checked out of the hostel, Tania went back to carnival for the day and I went looking for food - I found a nice little place where I got some amazing chicken fried rice ... For 2.95 I ended up with a plate that would feed 5 people!!! I got a doggy bag to eat the rest at the airport. I was looking forward to getting back home. My ride to the airport would leave in a couple hours - grabbed two boxes of that cheap wine for the road and headed back to the hostel. The flight home was incredibly scary but I made it back!
