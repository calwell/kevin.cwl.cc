The New, New Dominican in January 2011 - Part 3

Wed 26 01 2011

As a final part to these three parts I have written, I would like to make a compendium of pictures and videos gathered throughout the trip. These pictures were taken mainly by myself using a Canon t1i DLSR camera or using my smartphone (an HTC G2). Some fo these picture were also taken by my travel companion, Julio, using his compact digital camera. You may wish to skip much of the commentary and just go to the respective locations and browse through pictures and videos - by all means. For more visual delight, read on.


The Videos

In Punta Cana - I arrived to see an incredible sight - something I had never seen before! I was treated to that of a five star resort. Take a look at this amazing room and the amenities in it.

Some have said that this is nto so exciting - I disagree! THe sand in Punta Cana is just amazing. Take a look for yourself and decide.

Up in Puerto Plata, I took in a baseball with Davi. This game was so exciting that, at one point, the team and the crowd got into a big fight.

I was also able to get a ride on the famous cable car ride of Puerto Plata - this ride is about 6-7 minutes and take you through some of the most incredible scenery.
While I was generally (and mostly) staying in resort areas, I did manage to stay outside of the resort for a couple days. My good friend Davi was nice enough to let me stay at his house for a couple days. Here's a look at what I woke up too in the morning (while it was snowing at home!).
