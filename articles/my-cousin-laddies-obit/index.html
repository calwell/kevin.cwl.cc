My cousin Laddie's Obit...

Fri 21 01 2011

This was posted in the Toronto Sun on November 12, 2008:

BARNSWELL, Christopher “Laddie” McGregor -It is with great sadness that the family of Christopher Barnswell announce his passing on Nov. 8th, 2008. Born Mar. 13th, 1980, he was the cherished son of Nancy and Ted Petitpas and Collin Barnswell. Beloved brother of Corby Costain, Nikita Barnswell and Keisha Barnswell. He will be greatly missed by his grandparents, aunts, uncles, cousins, nieces, and nephews, all of whom he treasured dearly. He will be remembered for his kindness, sincerity, and heart of gold. Although Chris has passed, his loved ones know that he has found peace in his heart, and will cherish their memories of him always.Visitation will be held on Fri., Nov. 14th from 4-7:00p.m. A service will take place on Sat., Nov. 15th at 3:00p.m. Both will be held at the Trull Funeral Home and Cremation Centre, 1111 Danforth Ave., Toronto.
